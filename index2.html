Who We Are

Our Vision & Mission

We are a vibrant, Christ-centered church community in Mombasa, Kenya. We are dedicated to sharing, supporting, and growing in faith together. Our mission is to proclaim the Gospel, inspiring repentance, renewal, and a powerful spiritual legacy through worship, deep teaching, and community outreach.


Whether you are visiting Mombasa or searching for a spiritual home, our doors and our hearts are wide open to welcome you.
